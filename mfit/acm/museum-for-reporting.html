Museums for All Reporting

This information collection is being conducted in conformance with the Museum and Library Services Act of 2010, as amended. IMLS intends to make institutional participation information provided through this form publicly available. However, information specifically identifying any individual will be protected from public disclosure to the extent permitted by law.

The OMB control number, 3137-0089, expires on 03/31/2024. The Institute of Museum and Library Services may not conduct or sponsor, and a person is not required to respond to, an information collection unless it displays a currently valid OMB control number.

A children’s museum is defined as a nonprofit educational and cultural institution committed to serving the needs and interests of children by providing exhibits.
