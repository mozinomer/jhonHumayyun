Donate to ACM’s Impact Fund

The Association of Children’s Museums (ACM) is the only organization representing museums and professionals dedicated to early childhood play, the starting point in the continuum of lifelong learning.

Help support the programs and opportunities that ACM makes available each year through a gift to the ACM Impact Fund. By giving today, you help ACM support all children's museum professionals as we work to ensure a healthy start in life—physically, emotionally, socially, and intellectually—for all children. You’re also investing in ACM’s efforts to raise the profile of the children’s museum field.

Your contribution is tax deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

How Your Gift is Used

Data & Trends

ACM collects and analyzes data about children’s museums’ operations.

Scholarships

ACM underwrites the Diversity in Action scholarship, which supports InterActivity attendance by people of all backgrounds.

Webinars & Workshops

ACM presents regular webinars and workshops featuring experts from within and outside of the children’s museum field.

Advocacy

ACM educates elected officials and stakeholders about children’s museums’ impact on children,families, and communities.

Resource Library

ACM helps museum staff build their knowledge of core standards with fact sheets, sample documents, and toolkits.

Research

The Children’s Museum Research Network investigates, reports, and publishes about the learning values of children’s museums. .

Make Your Gift

When you make a gift to ACM, you are investing in the tools children’s museums use to offer high-quality, playful, educational experiences to every visitor. Please support ACM and help us ensure that the children’s museum field is the best it can be—not only this year but in every year to come.
